- **Headings:** Bold sans-serif (Space Grotesk, Montserrat, or Poppins Bold)
- **Body:** Clean sans-serif (Inter, Roboto, or Open Sans)
- **Size:** Large, readable text (minimum 40px for body, 60px+ for headings)

### Layout:
- **Consistent margins:** 80px padding on all sides
- **Visual hierarchy:** Large heading, supporting text below
- **Icons:** Simple, minimal, one per slide (optional)
- **Alignment:** Left-aligned text for readability

### Visual Style:
- **Neo-brutalist aesthetic:** Bold, high-contrast, thick borders (optional)
- **Minimal design:** Focus on typography, not decoration
- **Consistent branding:** Use same color palette across all carousels
- **Readable at thumbnail size:** Test visibility at small scale

### Tools for Creation:
- **Canva:** Use custom dimensions 1080x1350px
- **Figma:** Design system with reusable components
- **Adobe Express:** Quick templates with brand colors
- **Keynote/PowerPoint:** Export slides as images
